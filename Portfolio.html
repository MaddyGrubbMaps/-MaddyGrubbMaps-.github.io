Hand Drawn & Painted Maps

Blending the art of hand drawn illustration & digital coloring to create maps that really pop! Great for events, races, illustrative travel maps, tourism properties and long distance adventures!

THE FILM FEST

This was a poster I got to work on for TRIALS OF MILES - inspired by the beautiful and rugged Mount San Antoinio and the hills around Los Angeles.

COLORADO TRAIL

In the summer of 2020, I hiked the Colorado trail, as it runs 500 miles from Denver to Durango. This map is a tribute to the trail, and that experience. Prints available on Etsy!

Digital Mapping

The possibilities are endless! I always am trying to push myself and explore new visual styles digitally. Everything from city zoning maps to skin tracks high in the alpine. If you have a map idea, bring it on.

BLUE RIVER ECODUSTRIAL DISTRICT - KC

I partnered with a design firm and Kansas City to develop a new map set that better reflects the values of the new urban plan. Connecting nature, industry and community, I used green earth tones, and darker, blues and greys to express the connections they were trying to make, and the customers they were hoping to attract.

USGS - LAKE CHELAN 1968

This was a personal project I tried out just for kicks. A very popular art style these days that bridges the gap between historic topo maps and new cutting edge methods of modelling relief. The basemap is a 1968 USGS 7.5 min quad of Manson WA, and the shaded relief was modelled in Blender and compiled in photoshop.

YELLOWSTONE NATNL. PARK

This map was created for Home Decor Maps, in a style similar to the 50 state series we had partnered on for the last 2 years. It's meant to be modern, elegant, and minimalistic. Something that people want to hang up on their walls. Terrain and major roads are shown, physical features are labelled and that's pretty much it.

PICO DE ORIZABA (CITLALTÉPETL)

Reference map of the area around Mexico's tallest peak (and North America's 3rd). Landcover data, satellite imagery, and elevation were combined to create a basemap that expresses the regions' unique topography and weather patterns.

WASATCH WINTER

Created in the fall of 2021 as a donation piece for the Utah Avalanche Center. The base of this map was hand painted with acrylics, and it is a great example of how art and real world data can be combined through the medium of cartography. And! Over $2500 in profits have been donated to the UAC since the print was released!

MOUNT ROBSON & BERG LAKE

This was a fun little map series for a client who wanted to commemorate 3 summer backpacking trips through the medium of maps. He wanted them to be colorful, minimalistic and only show what was special about that adventure. A custom mountain icon was also drawn for each map.

BEND OREGON

Bend! It's such a cute little town, with such incredibly easy access to outdoor spaces. I grew up climbing and skiing in Bend, and my parents are in the process of moving down there (like everyone else and their dog). This map was originally a gift to them, trying to capture the character of the city, while also helping them get a sense of place. but it is now also on sale on Etsy, and this style has since become a series.

GLACIERS OF THE CASCADE VOLCANOES

The cascades are my happy place, and glaciers are on of the things I get most stoked about scientifcally. This print, just about sums all those passions up. It also includes native names of all the peaks, as they were known before homesteaders arrived and took the land.

OLD GROWTH

A comprehensive map that summed up global data from various sources, to show current remaining old growth forests, and what is being threatened by human encroachment.

Scientific Research and Geospatial Analysis

Social and Physical science research through GIS analysis and spatial statistics. I am particularly passionate in cartography for Earth Sciences (water/ice resources, geology, gemorphology, energy, ecology, fire) but am available for any type of place based anaylsis work! Experienced in industry standard (ArcGIS Pro, Adobe, etc.) as well as open source software (QGIS, Mapbox, Python, R)

BEARS EARS

A research project conducted on the recent boundary changes in the BENM region and what geographic effects those changes could have on Native Archeological sites, Biodiversity, and Paleontological importance.

BEARS EARS #2
